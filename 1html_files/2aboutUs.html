Rujeko (Light) NPO organization that was started with the intention to create safe homes for girs that are rescued from child marriages all over Africa.We want every child , especially the girl child to have a fair chance at being educated .

Addressing the Escalating Issue of Child Marriages in Niger

In recent years, Niger has witnessed an alarming surge in the prevalence of child marriages. As a consequence, Rujeko has undertaken a crucial mission to rescue and provide shelter for these vulnerable girls. However, the endeavor has encountered significant obstacles due to the scarcity of donations. Consequently, a concerted effort is being made to prioritize farming activities as a means to sustain the mission's objectives.
